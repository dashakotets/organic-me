ABOUT US

Welcome to OrganicMe! Our journey began with a simple but powerful idea: to live in harmony with nature and share that passion with others. As a family, we’ve always valued clean, organic living—whether it’s the food we eat, the products we use, or the way we care for our home.

OrganicME

It all started in our cozy kitchen in Scotland’s Borders, where we dreamed of creating a business that reflected our values: sustainability, health, and connection to the natural world. What began as a small family project has blossomed into a thriving community of like-minded individuals who care about making mindful choices for a better planet.

As a family-owned business, every product we offer is carefully chosen and lovingly tested. From the sweet honey crafted by our buzzing partners to the eco-friendly household items that brighten your day, everything we do comes with a personal touch and a commitment to quality.
