ORGANIC ME

At OrganicMe, we’re committed to offering high-quality organic food, eco-friendly household products, and sustainable homeware. Based in the heart of Scotland’s Borders, we inspire you to make mindful choices that benefit both your health and the planet. Join us in living a more natural, balanced lifestyle—where every product is a step toward a greener future.

OUR HONEY

Un-bee-lievably Good: Sweetness You Can Trust!

Our honey is a true gift from nature—crafted with care by bees that forage in pristine, untouched meadows. Rich in flavor and packed with nutrients, it’s not just a treat for your taste buds but a boost for your well-being. Uniquely pure and naturally harvested, every jar embodies the harmony of organic living and the essence of sustainable beekeeping. Savor the sweetness of a healthier, happier planet.

Meet the Stars of Our Hive

At OrganicMe, our honey is made by the hardest-working team in nature—our incredible bees!

Led by Queen Buzzella, they roam the vibrant wildflowers of Scotland’s Borders, collecting nectar and spreading life wherever they go.

Each jar of honey is the result of their tireless dedication and teamwork. From the speedy scouts who find the best flowers to the skilled workers who craft the golden goodness, every bee plays a part in creating something truly special.
