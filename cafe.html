OUR CAFE

Welcome to the OrganicMe Café—a cozy corner where sustainability meets flavor! Nestled in the heart of Scotland’s Borders, our café is an extension of our commitment to organic living and eco-friendly practices.

We serve freshly brewed organic coffee, herbal teas, and a selection of wholesome, homemade treats. From locally sourced ingredients to plant-based options, every item on our menu is crafted with care to nourish your body and soul. Whether you’re here for a quick cup of coffee, a leisurely brunch, or just to relax with friends, our café offers a warm, welcoming space where you can unwind and enjoy the simple pleasures of life.
